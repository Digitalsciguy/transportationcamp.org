JANUARY 6-7, 2018 MARKS THE FIRST ANNUAL

TRANSPORTATION WEEKEND!

Kickoff transportation’s biggest month of the year
by learning and networking all weekend long!

SATURDAY JANUARY 6TH ACTIVITIES ($50 REGISTRATION FEE)
George Mason University, Arlington Campus (hosted by MobilityLab)

TransportationCamp DC (8:30 a.m. – 8:00 p.m.)
Join more than 400 industry thought leaders, young professionals, and students
from around the country as they participate in transportation-related sessions that
foster the exchange of innovative ideas in a high-energy environment.

SUNDAY JANUARY 7TH ACTIVITIES ($50 REGISTRATION FEE)*
Walter E. Washington Convention Center, Washington DC (hosted by TRB)

Careers in Motion Networking Fair (10:00 a.m. – 2:00 p.m.)
In partnership with WTS, Eno, Young Professionals in Transportation (YPT), and
COMTO, this event will provide an opportunity for attendees to meet and network
with prospective employers from a wide range of multimodal transportation-
related disciplines.
Welcome and Attendee Orientation Session (2:30 p.m. – 4:00 p.m.)
This session provides helpful information about how to become involved in TRB
committee activities and how attendees can make the best use of the time at the
TRB Annual Meeting.
Exhibit Hall Opening and Reception (4:00 p.m. – 7:00 p.m.)
Attendees will learn about a variety of multimodal transportation-related products
and services showcased by commercial organizations and TRB Sponsors in a fun
and vibrant environment.
*Registrants of the 2018 TRB Annual Meeting will have full access to Sunday events
